Our Story

"A home is built one good find at a time."

Tiana's Heirloom Haven started in Abule Egba with a simple idea — that the things you live with should mean something. Not just jewelry, appliances or shoes, but pieces with a bit of presence. The kind people ask about.

Every category in the haven, from kitchen finds to statement jewelry, is sourced and sorted the same way: with an eye for what lasts, and a soft spot for things that feel a little special.

If you don't see exactly what you're after, send us a message. Chances are, it's somewhere in the haven.
